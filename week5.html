Best Dogs !

Huskies

Huskies are very energetic dogs. They were orginally bred as a working dog, and it is believed that they originated from the Chukchi, a tribe in siberia.

Facts About Huskies!!

  • Related to Wolves
  • One of the most energetic dog breed
  • Have an 12-15 year life expectancy
  • Don't get fatigued
  • Are very friendly and playful
  • They love to howl

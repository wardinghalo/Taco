About German Sheperds

According to Dogtime.com They excel at mostly anything they are trained to do a few examples being; search and rescue, drug detection, and assistance work for the handicapped.

  • Originated in Germany
  • Life expectancy 9-13 years
  • Males weigh 66-88 lbs while females weigh 49-71 lbs

      • German Sheperd Traits

    1. Confident
    2. Courageous
    3. Smart
